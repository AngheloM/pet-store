Bird

A bird is an endothermic vertebrate that is able to fly because it has wings, feathers, and a strong lightweight skeleton.

Cat

Cats are a domestic carnivorous mammals that are typically furry, and have similar features to a lion.

Dog

A dog is a domesticated mammal that were bred from wolves, and have similar features to a wolf.

Horse

Horses have 4 limbs, and aren't usually rasied for meat. They are used for pleasure, and service, such as riding, pulling carriages, and etc.

Turtle

Turtles are reptiles that have a hard shell on their back that they use as a shield. They are cold blooded, and live near, or in the water.
